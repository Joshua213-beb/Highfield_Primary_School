Mrs Getrude Mutanda

Head Teacher

As the Headteacher of Highfield Schools, it is my pleasure to welcome you to our school’s website. At our school, we pride ourselves on maintaining a reasonable number of enrolment. This allows our dedicated staff to provide personalized attention to each pupil, ensuring that every child is known, valued, and supported in their learning journey. We believe that our smaller class sizes offer a unique advantage, allowing teachers to connect with pupils on an individual level. This personalized approach enables us to understand each pupil’s strengths, challenges, and interests, tailoring our teaching methods to help them reach their full potential.

  • Health Services

    The school runs a sickbay, which takes care of minor ailments for pupils. Major cases are referred to the parents. Complicated cases are referred to hospital via parent/guardian.

  • Music Dance and Drama

    we celebrate the rich talents of our students through our vibrant Music, Dance, and Drama (MDD) program. MDD plays an essential role in our curriculum, encouraging students to express themselves creatively, build confidence, and explore diverse cultures.

  • Educational Trips

    we believe that learning extends beyond the classroom. Our educational trips offer students the opportunity to explore new environments, experience different cultures, and gain practical knowledge ."

  • Co-curricular

    Through sports, students learn the importance of discipline, perseverance, and fair play, while also enjoying the health benefits of physical activity. At Highfield, we believe that sports not only enhance physical fitness but also inspire leadership, confidence, and a strong sense of community."

  • Agriculture

    Our agriculture program provides students with hands-on learning experiences in gardening, crop cultivation, and animal care. Through this program, students gain practical skills in food production and an understanding of environmental stewardship. By working in our school gardens, students learn about responsibility, teamwork, and the value of hard work.

  • Church

    Highfield Primary School Gayaza is rooted in the values and beliefs of the Seventh-day Adventist Church. Our commitment to faith shapes our approach to education, emphasizing character development, compassion, and a strong moral foundation. Through regular worship, Bible study, and spiritual guidance, we encourage students to deepen their relationship with God and to live out Christian principles in their daily lives.
