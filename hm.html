Mrs Getrude Mutanda

Head Teacher

As the Headteacher of Highfield Schools, it is my pleasure to welcome you to our school’s website. At our school, we pride ourselves on maintaining a reasonable number of enrolment. This allows our dedicated staff to provide personalized attention to each pupil, ensuring that every child is known, valued, and supported in their learning journey. We believe that our smaller class sizes offer a unique advantage, allowing teachers to connect with pupils on an individual level. This personalized approach enables us to understand each pupil’s strengths, challenges, and interests, tailoring our teaching methods to help them reach their full potential.

In addition, we aim to create a safe, secure and sustainable learning environment where pupils feel valued and happy. Our pupils are encouraged, praised and taught to be responsible citizens, and take an active role in their community. We instill in them the importance of moral values such as honesty, respect, reliable, faithfully transparent, and personal responsibility.

Our Aim

In partnership with parents, we seek to ensure all students:

  • Receive an excellent and rich education, achieving to the best of their ability.
  • Are happy, healthy, confident, independent, and motivated learners.
  • Have a strong foundation of values to use to make informed choices in a local and global community.
  • Are able to fit into a society that awaits them in Uganda and on their travels abroad.
  • Develop a love for learning and are motivated by education.
  • Are developed as individuals, and their diverse strengths, cultural backgrounds, gifts, and aspirations are celebrated.

We invite you to explore our website to learn more about our programs, values, and the enriching experiences we offer. Thank you for visiting, and we look forward to welcoming you to our school community.
